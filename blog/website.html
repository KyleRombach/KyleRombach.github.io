Making a Website
2/28/2016

I have to say making a website is really fun, yet challenging. It's cool to see I have a functioning site but my ability to write CSS isn't where I want it to be. I wish I was more creative and had some cool layouts to give my website a little more flair. I've definitely enjoyed seeing my progress and how I've gotten better with my coding to implement some of the ideas I have for my site. It's been a ton of trial and error to get some things positioned right but every time I try something new, it gives me more insight into what works and what doesn't. One day I'll probably look back at an original look of my website and see how I've grown and changed it from it's beginning.
